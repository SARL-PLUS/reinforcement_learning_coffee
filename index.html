About The RL Coffee

The Reinforcement Learning Coffee is an informal meetup held on the first Friday of every month. The entry level is intentionally low and everyone is welcome—from newcomers to experienced practitioners interested in reinforcement learning and sequential decision-making. Topics can range from an initial idea or open problem to a more involved project, your latest paper or a paper idea, or simply trying to understand a recent paper together.

The idea was born out of the Machine Learning Coffee we introduced at CERN, where we introduced machine learning and reinforcement learning to the CERN accelerator complex.
